Initial Deposit Bonus
A cash bonus that an online poker room gives tonew players that sign up and make a deposit. Typically, the poker room makes the bonus a percentage of the player's first deposit. Also known as a Sign Up Bonus.
Inside Straight Draw
A draw to a straight that is missing one of the cards in the middle (as opposed to on the end).
