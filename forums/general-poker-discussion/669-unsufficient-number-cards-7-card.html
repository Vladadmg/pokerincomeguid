unsufficient number of cards at 7 card stud poker at 8 players table?
At 7 cards stud poker each player can have up to 7 cards if they call each hand and 8 players are allowed to play at a table simultaneously. So 7*8= 56 cards can be used by players at a table in one hand. But poker is played by only one deck and there are 52 cards in a deck as we all know. So what will happen if all the players call until the 7th street (last betting round)? There will be insufficient number of cards. Wont there be? How will this problem be solved?
