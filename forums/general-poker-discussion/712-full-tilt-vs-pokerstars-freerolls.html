My answer is neither. You're far better off playing micro entry tournaments where there is a real prize pool. Freerolls are for entertainment purposes only and the expected value even for very good players is a few pennies for an hour or two of work. Not a good return for your time invested. So like I said it's for entertainment. Poker pro Chris Ferguson played these for many months before he was able to win a couple bucks and I do mean a couple bucks. He was out to show how you can build a poker roll from nothing but the lesson was that you really need to avoid trying to do this no matter how good you are. You could get a paper route and use the pay for one week to get at a level of money you would take years to get to by playing these turkeys.King Cobra Pokerhttp://kingcobrapoker.com
