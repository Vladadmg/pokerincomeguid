I'm not familiar with the particular site but with all gambling deposit bonuses there is always terms and conditions. Usually it involves having to turnover your initial deposit before they release the bonus amount, or they'll have a points system associated with it, where you earn points each time you bet and when you reach a certain amount of points your bonus will be released.. that sort of thing.These sorts of bonus deposits are always good for the player, but they're not just going to give you absolutely free money if that's what you're thinking. If there were no release terms then everyone would just be depositing the $500, then withdrawing $800 straight away and making $300 a day (it would be nice!).
