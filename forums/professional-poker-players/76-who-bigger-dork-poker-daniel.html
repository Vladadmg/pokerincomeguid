I think Negreanu is fun to watch, but maybe that's because I'm a dork myself. I have never seen Daniel be malicious in any way, except possibly to Matusow, and even that's all in fun. Jamie Gold, on the other hand, goes so far beyond "dork". All his talk of trapping when he didn't even have a made hand, telling other people at the table what he had (top-top comes instantly to mind), refusing to pay off the deals he made, and just his general smirky arrogant attitude makes me want to send him a live rattlesnake in the mail. He's talk talk talk talk talk, and none of it is even amusing. He and Helmuth should be forced to play a game where the loser is banned from poker forever and the winner gets his mouth surgically sewn shut. Johnny Chan should have done us a favor and squashed him like a bug, instead he took him under his wing.
