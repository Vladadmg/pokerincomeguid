Well, in a game like Texas Hold'em, the worst two-card "starting hand" is 2-7 offsuit. The best starting hand is a Pair of Aces (AA).

Like Chris already mentioned, the worst five card hand is something like;
A-2-10-7-Q - high card only with a mix of suits. From there, the order of poker hands goes;
  • One Pair
  • Two Pair
  • Three-of-a-kind
  • Straight
  • Flush
  • Full House
  • Four-of-a-kind
  • Straight Flush
  • Royal Flush
